1298: <h3>7.3 Closing summary (usable directly as a defense opening or closing statement)</h3>
1299: <p>This suite is a layered set of diagnostics, not an assumption-free theorem chain. <b>RMSE/MAE/Bias/P95</b> summarize selected location, magnitude, direction, and tail depths; <b>coverage</b> reports interval hits at declared levels; <b>PIT/reliability/ECE</b> probe broader calibration patterns. <b>CRPS</b> strict propriety requires the finite-first-moment score class and a common evaluation population and unit. <b>Gaussian NLL</b> moment targeting requires a well-defined finite log-risk, positive finite conditional variance, and an admissible function class; the clipped network implementation has the separate boundary in §5.1. <b>LONO</b> is an independent geometry-holdout protocol, not a scoring theorem or a sampling-standard-error construction. Each empirical claim additionally requires its pinned population, computation, and uncertainty convention. The metrics have overlapping rather than perfectly complementary blind spots. <b>Part II (§8–§13) develops conditional analogies to financial-risk tools; an analogy transfers only after its own time-series, likelihood, and decision assumptions are stated.</b></p>

1317: <h3>8.1 The Sharpe ratio and its standard error</h3>
1318: <p>Let per-period (say daily) excess strategy returns \(r_1,\dots,r_T\) have mean \(\mu\) and standard deviation \(\sigma\), with \(0&lt;\sigma^2&lt;\infty\). The Sharpe ratio and its natural estimator are</p>
1319: \[
1320: \mathrm{SR}=\frac{\mu}{\sigma},\qquad
1321: \widehat{\mathrm{SR}}=\frac{\hat\mu}{\hat\sigma},\qquad
1322: \hat\mu=\frac1T\sum_t r_t,\quad \hat\sigma^2=\frac1T\sum_t (r_t-\hat\mu)^2 .
1323: \tag{8.1}
1324: \]
1325: <div class="lem"><span class="label">Lemma 8.1 (standard error of the Sharpe ratio; Lo 2002).</span>
1326: If \(r_t\overset{\mathrm{iid}}{\sim}\mathcal N(\mu,\sigma^2)\), then as \(T\to\infty\)
1327: \[
1328: \sqrt T\bigl(\widehat{\mathrm{SR}}-\mathrm{SR}\bigr)\Rightarrow
1329: \mathcal N\!\left(0,1+\tfrac12\mathrm{SR}^2\right),\qquad
1330: \Var(\widehat{\mathrm{SR}})=\frac{1+\tfrac12\mathrm{SR}^2}{T}+o(T^{-1}).

1313: <h2 id="sec8">§8 回測統計：有效樣本數、Sharpe 標準誤與多重檢定</h2>
1314: 
1315: <p><b>第二部從這裡開始。</b>每一章都從第一部已證明的結果出發，展示量化金融怎麼稱呼它。本章直接長在 §0.3 上：\(T\) 個回測日不一定提供 \(T\) 個獨立觀測單位；不重疊的 iid returns 可獨立，但 serial dependence 或 overlap 會改變各統計量自身的 long-run variance。跨策略搜索則是另一個 joint-null multiplicity 問題，不是 time-series effective sample size 的縮減。</p>

1317: <h3>8.1 Sharpe 比率及其標準誤</h3>
1318: <p>設每期（例如每日）超額策略收益 \(r_1,\dots,r_T\) 的均值為 \(\mu\)、標準差為 \(\sigma\)，且 \(0&lt;\sigma^2&lt;\infty\)。Sharpe 比率與其自然估計量為</p>
1319: \[
1320: \mathrm{SR}=\frac{\mu}{\sigma},\qquad
1321: \widehat{\mathrm{SR}}=\frac{\hat\mu}{\hat\sigma},\qquad
1322: \hat\mu=\frac1T\sum_t r_t,\quad \hat\sigma^2=\frac1T\sum_t (r_t-\hat\mu)^2 .
1323: \tag{8.1}
1324: \]
1325: <div class="lem"><span class="label">引理 8.1（Sharpe 比率的標準誤；Lo 2002）.</span>
1326: 若 \(r_t\overset{\mathrm{iid}}{\sim}\mathcal N(\mu,\sigma^2)\)，則當 \(T\to\infty\)
1327: \[
1328: \sqrt T\bigl(\widehat{\mathrm{SR}}-\mathrm{SR}\bigr)\Rightarrow
1329: \mathcal N\!\left(0,1+\tfrac12\mathrm{SR}^2\right),\qquad
1330: \Var(\widehat{\mathrm{SR}})=\frac{1+\tfrac12\mathrm{SR}^2}{T}+o(T^{-1}).
